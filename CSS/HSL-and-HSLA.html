HSL-and-HSLA

What the fuck is that?

So HSL stands for Hue, Saturation and Lightness. So the three parameters would look something like:

background-color: hsl(180,30%,50%);

And that's what it looks like.

HSLA stands for Hue, Saturation, Lightness and Alpha. So those parameters would look like:

background-color: hsla(30,60%,75%,0.5);
