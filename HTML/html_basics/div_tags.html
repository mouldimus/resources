Div Tags

Why are div tags used?

A div tag isn't too different from a p tag, except, there are a few major differences.
1. divs are nestable, meaning they can be the parent/child elements to other div tags. Whereas p tags would just pop out of each and this would cause problems.
2. the spacing between block elements is different, theres less space
